Overview

Soleguard Alliance is a Kenyan-based non-governmental organization (NGO) committed to empowering communities through the eradication of jigger infestations, promoting animal welfare, fostering environmental conservation, and advancing the blue economy. Operating from Nairobi, Nairobi, the organization was founded with a vision to build resilient, healthy, and prosperous communities. Soleguard Alliance's work is driven by the belief that sustainable solutions to community challenges can only be achieved through a combination of public awareness, collaboration, and practical interventions.

About us - key pillars

Jigger Eradication and Public Health

Jiggers are a pervasive health problem affecting vulnerable populations, especially in rural areas. Soleguard Alliance works to eradicate jigger infestations by promoting awareness, implementing preventive measures, providing treatment, and mobilizing communities to take collective action against this debilitating condition.

Learn more about Jiggers

Animal Welfare

Soleguard Alliance advocates for the humane treatment of animals and promotes animal welfare by raising awareness, improving veterinary services, and working with communities to ensure proper care for livestock, companion animals, and wildlife.

Learn more about animal welfare

Environmental Conservation

Environmental degradation threatens the livelihoods and health of many communities in Kenya. Soleguard Alliance focuses on environmental conservation by promoting sustainable practices, restoring ecosystems, and raising awareness about the importance of protecting natural resources.

Learn more about environmental conservation
